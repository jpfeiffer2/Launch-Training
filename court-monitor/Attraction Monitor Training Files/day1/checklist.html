Readiness

Arrives in proper uniform with name badge visible
Has whistle, working walkie, and First Aid bag as required
Demonstrates professional floor presence without phone, gum, or leaning

Core Monitoring Skills

Maintains active visual scanning and remains focused on the assigned attraction zone
Positions themselves so the entire assigned area can be effectively monitored
Checks wristbands, access restrictions, jump time, and Launch Grip Socks correctly
Recognizes unsafe behavior without relying on the trainer to point it out

Guest Interaction

Corrects unsafe guest behavior promptly using a friendly, firm, and professional approach
Clearly explains the rule and connects the correction to guest safety
Uses whistle appropriately without overusing it
Calls management for repeated rule breaking, difficult guest behavior, or unresolved safety concerns

Coverage

Does not leave an attraction unattended
Radios for coverage and waits until replacement arrives before leaving the assigned zone
